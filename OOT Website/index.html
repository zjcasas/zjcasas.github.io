About

When we were in college, we were all members of an a cappella group called The MIT/Wellesley Toons. After graduating, we all missed singing so much that we decided to start an alum group called the Out of Toons in September 2014. We are born out of a pure love for singing together in harmony. None of us are professional musicians; as a matter of fact, our professions could not be more different. Our members consist of engineers, computer programmers, and arts professionals, but we are united under our passion for music, performance, and colorful bow ties.
