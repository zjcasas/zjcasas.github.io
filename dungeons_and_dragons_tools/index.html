About

Welcome to D&D Tools! D&D Tools provides players with tools and information to assist them in creating new characters and level up their existing characters in Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition. Stay tuned for future updates. Things I'd like to add include pages for equipment, backgrounds, and general rules for playing, character name generation, an encounter experience point calculator, random character generation, character management, and potentially more.
